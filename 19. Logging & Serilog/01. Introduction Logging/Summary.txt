Logging
  is the process of recording run-time actions as they happen in real-time.
  Helps us to understand the failures and performance bottlenecks of the application.

  .NET Core App ---> Logging Framework ---> Logs (Console, Debug, EventLog, File, Database)

  Logging Framework is the mediator between your application and the Logs
  Logs are data store where the actual log messages are persisted.
